FAQ

Have Any Question! We Are Here To Answer You

  • How to register for cleaning services?

    To register for our professional cleaning services, simply scroll to the top of the page, locate the "book a schedule" button, and click on it. Fill in the form with your details, including your preferred schedule and any specific requirements you may have. Once submitted, one of our representatives will promptly contact you to confirm your booking and discuss any further details. We look forward to providing you with our exceptional cleaning services tailored to your need.

  • How to join our team of cleaners?

    To join our team of dedicated cleaners, please follow these simple steps: scroll to the top of the page, click on "services," and then select "recruitment." Fill out the form provided, and proceed to pay the professional CV writing fee and training manual fee of only R60.00. Once your payment and form submission are complete, one of our representatives will promptly contact you to assist you further in the recruitment process. We look forward to welcoming you to our team of professionals committed to delivering top-notch cleaning services.

  • Adjust a schedule for cleaning?

    To adjust your cleaning schedule, simply scroll to the bottom of the page where you'll find our WhatsApp number/Facebook link. Contact us via these channels, and one of our representatives will promptly assist you in rescheduling your cleaning appointments according to your convenience. We are committed to ensuring your cleaning needs are met efficiently and effectively.

  • Any money return guarenty for unsatisfy service?

    For inquiries regarding our money-back guarantee for unsatisfactory service, please scroll to the bottom of the page to find our WhatsApp number/Facebook link. Contact us via these channels within 12 hours of service delivery to make a claim. One of our representatives will promptly assist you and guide you through the refund process as per our policy. We are committed to ensuring your satisfaction with our professional cleaning services and addressing any concerns you may have effectively.

  • Need a pricing plan?

    To inquire about our pricing plans, please scroll to the bottom of the page where you can find our WhatsApp number/Facebook link or email address. Contact us via WhatsApp number/Facebook or send us an email, and one of our representatives will promptly get in touch with you to discuss our pricing options for our professional cleaning services. We are here to assist you in selecting the right plan that meets your cleaning needs efficiently and affordably.

  • What if something is broken during cleaning?

    In the event of any damage during cleaning, please scroll to the bottom of the page to find our WhatsApp number/Facebook link. Contact us via WhatsApp number/Facebook within 48 hours of service delivery, providing images of the damage. One of our representatives will promptly assist you in addressing the issue and guide you through our claims process. We prioritize resolving any incidents swiftly and ensuring your satisfaction with our professional cleaning services.

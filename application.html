Recruitment Instructions and Requirements

To apply for a position with Clean Slate, applicants must meet the following criteria: possess a South African ID, passport, or a valid work permit. Here's how to apply:

Step 1: Access the Job Application Form

  • Navigate to the top of the page and click on "Jobs" in the navigation bar.
  • Fill out all the job application questions and upload your CV.

Step 2: Complete the Payment

  • Proceed to "Payments" under "Jobs" in the navigation bar.
  • Pay the R60.00 once-off fee either through the payment gateway or via EFT to our bank account. Remember to use your full names and surname as the reference.
  • Note: Applications will not be processed until payment is completed.

Step 3: Await Contact from Recruitment Agent

  • Once payment is confirmed, one of our recruitment agents will contact you regarding placement information.

Step 4: Receive Your Training Manual

  • After your application is processed, you will receive your training manual via email.
